This platformer project was made in the course of 2 months with a team of 12 people : 3 gameplay programmers, 7 game artists and 2 sound designer.

You play as a puppet dwelling in a cabinet of curiosities, awakened by the soul of its owner. The game's unique mechanic allows this soul to travel freely across puppets, changing the character you control. This creates engaging puzzle situations centered around interactions with the surrounding environment that enhance the platforming gameplay.

The game features two levels with distinct atmospheres and design intentions. The first is calmer, emphasizing curiosity and reflection, while in the second, curiosity gives way to apprehension as things become stranger and more unsettling.

I learned a lot during this project :

- Implementation of a hand-made physics system for character movements, with a range of various tweakable paramaters.

- Tweaking the character control to reach our desired gamefeel.

- Implementation of a hand-made collisions system using raycasts.

- Implementation of multiple interactive objects, such as the scale and the piano (cf. video)

- Complete level design of the first level, with multiple puzzle and platforming situations and a focus on camera settings.

- Close collaboration with game artist and sound designer during all phases of the project : conception, level design, implementation of interactive objects...

- Juicy effects such as speedlines and dust particles.
